Awareness Campaigns

Join our efforts to reduce food waste and promote sustainability in households and communities. Participate in our campaigns and help spread awareness about the importance of responsible food consumption.

Our Campaigns

Food Recovery Challenge

Join our efforts to recover surplus food from local businesses and distribute it to those in need, minimizing food waste.

Community Gardens Initiative

Participate in creating community gardens to grow fresh produce, reducing food waste and promoting healthy eating.

Creative Leftover Cooking

Learn and share recipes that use leftovers creatively, reducing food waste in households and inspiring others.

Food Waste Education Workshops

Attend workshops to learn about food waste issues, sustainable practices, and how you can make a difference in your community.
